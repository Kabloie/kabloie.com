Company

The design of the Kabloie logo was inspired by the continuously looping möbius strip.

We believe merging the digital and physical will be the next evolution of technology. This means that the way people interract with technology will have to be completely redesigned.

That's why we started Kabloie, a technology and design company.

Our mission is to identify the very edge of technological advancements, then innovate, design and build at that intersection in order to push humanity forward.

Team

Tobías van Oudheusden and Antonio Divković

Antonio and Tobías met as classmates at the Master’s program Entrepreneurship and Innovation at Lund University, where they quickly connected over the idea for Kabloie.

Tobías van Oudheusden

Tobías holds a BSc and MSc in Product Development from the University of Antwerp and is a passionate product designer. His work has received multiple awards, including one from Dyson, and has been exhibited at leading international design fairs. For Kabloie, Tobías is responsible for building the physical side of things, i.e. the smart ring, as well as branding and graphic design.

Antonio Divković

Antonio holds a BSc in Business and Economics from Lund University and is a self-taught programmer with several years of experience building websites and mobile apps. One of his most recently launched apps, A4: Type, Swipe, Share, has 400+ monthly active users and growing. Antonio is responsible for building the digital side of things for Kabloie, i.e. the app and backend infrastructure.

Both founders recently graduated with an MSc in Entrepreneurship and Innovation from Lund University.
